Strategy Logic — NY Session 4H Level + 5M Flag/Reclaim (Robinhood Live Execution)
Step 1 · Find NY Session 4h Level
• Find the first 4h candle of the New York session each day
• NY session 4h candle = opens at 12:00 UTC, closes at 16:00 UTC
• LevelHigh = High of that candle | LevelLow = Low of that candle
Price data sourced from Kraken (public API). Orders execute on Robinhood.
Step 2 · Find FLAG Candle (after 16:00 UTC)
• After the 4h candle closes, switch to the 5-minute chart
• Watch for a 5m candle whose close < LevelLow
• That candle becomes the FLAG candle
Step 3 · BUY Entry (LevelLow Reclaim)
• Watch for a 5m candle that closes > LevelLow
• In live mode: Robinhood market buy order placed immediately
• SL = Lowest low from FLAG candle → entry candle
• Risk per trade: 2% of balance
Step 4 · Decremental SL Trail (5s checks)
• 1R profit → SL = entry (breakeven)
• 1.5R profit → SL = entry + 1×dist (1R locked)
• 2R profit → Phase 4 trail: SL trails max−min($25, 0.25×dist)
• When SL hit: Robinhood market sell order placed automatically
